1. Introduction

Welcome to RespireAI ("we," "our," or "us"). RespireAI is an AI‑powered respiratory health monitoring application designed to help patients and healthcare providers track, analyze, and manage respiratory health data.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal and health information when you use our mobile application, web platform, and related services (collectively, the "Services"). By using RespireAI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Medical Disclaimer: RespireAI is intended for health monitoring and informational purposes only. It does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for medical decisions.

2. Data We Collect

a) Personal Information

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Date of birth, gender, and profile photo (optional)
  • Account credentials (password stored in hashed form)
  • Role designation (Patient or Healthcare Provider)

b) Health & Vital Signs Data

  • Respiratory rate, SpO₂ (blood oxygen saturation), and heart rate
  • Temperature, blood pressure, and peak flow readings
  • Height, weight, BMI, and related body metrics
  • Data synced from Apple HealthKit (with your explicit permission)

c) Usage & Device Data

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Interaction logs (features used, session duration)
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Health Monitoring: Display your vital signs trends and historical data for self-tracking and doctor review
  • AI-Powered Risk Assessment: Analyze your vitals using machine learning models to generate personalized respiratory risk scores and early warning alerts
  • Doctor-Patient Connectivity: Share your health records securely with your assigned healthcare providers for remote consultations
  • Notifications & Alerts: Send timely alerts when your vitals indicate potential health concerns
  • Service Improvement: Aggregate anonymized data to enhance our AI models and improve overall app experience
  • Account Management: Authenticate users, process password changes, and manage account preferences

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal or health data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Your Healthcare Providers: When you explicitly link with a doctor through the app, relevant health data is shared to facilitate care
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors who help us operate the service (hosting, email delivery) under strict data protection agreements
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
  • Safety: To protect the rights, safety, or property of RespireAI, our users, or the public

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt; JWT-based session management ensures secure access
  • Access Control: Role-based access ensures that only authorized healthcare providers can view patient records
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • HIPAA Alignment: Our practices are designed to align with HIPAA security and privacy standards for health data
While we take every reasonable precaution to protect your data, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal and health data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Services. Specifically:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained throughout the period your account remains active
  • Account Deletion: Upon account deletion, all personal and health data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, de-identified data used for research and service improvement may be retained indefinitely

7. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. As a RespireAI user, you may:

  • Access: View all personal and health data stored in your account at any time through the app
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information via your Profile settings
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data from Settings → Delete Account
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your health records in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke HealthKit permissions or disable data sharing at any time through your device settings
  • Opt-Out: Disable AI alerts and notifications from App Preferences

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@respireai.com.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate or interact with the following third-party services:

  • Apple HealthKit: With your permission, we read and sync health data from HealthKit. We do not share HealthKit data with third parties or use it for advertising
  • Email Services (SMTP): Used solely for account verification, password resets, and critical health alerts
  • Cloud Hosting: Data is stored on secure, industry-standard cloud infrastructure

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies for full details.

9. Children's Privacy

RespireAI is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will take immediate steps to delete such information. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • We will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy
  • We will notify you via in-app notification or email
  • Continued use of the Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
